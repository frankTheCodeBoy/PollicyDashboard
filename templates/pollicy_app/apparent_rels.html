Are there noticeable links between the perpetrators and their victims?

Using network analysis, the accounts posting comments or replies to each gender were mapped out by strategic density comparison. The diagram below shows the differential variation in responses to the two genders.

It is evident that densely populated cluster (at 0.68 square inches) represents the female victims in this scenario while the remainder (at 0.32 square inches) represent male victims. This visualisation evidences the fact that the females are major victims to online targeted violence.

Gendered Network Visualisation Showing Large Bias Toward Female-Targeted Online Violence.
