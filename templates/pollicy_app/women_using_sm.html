Our study found that nine out of every ten (89%) accounts were used to put out at least one tweet/post during the campaign period. Twitter was the most widely used platform with92% of the candidates using the platform at least once during the monitoring period while Facebook had 86%. There was a considerable drop in social media usage during the month of January, which is greatly attributable to the internet shutdown in Uganda. In terms of gender, women were most active on Facebook while men dominated usage on Twitter. At least 69% of the accounts belonging to female candidates on Facebook were used at least once each week compared to the men at 47.5%.
